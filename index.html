Welcome to Beetlebites

Beetlebites is more than a restaurant; it's a culinary cabinet of curiosities. Tucked away in a vine-covered building, this eatery invites you into a world where gothic charm meets inventive gastronomy.

The menu is a celebration of the unexpected. Our chefs craft bold, seasonally-inspired dishes that transform simple, locally-sourced ingredients into edible art. From our signature "Moth Wings" - a surprising take on crispy poultry - to the rich, earthy flavors of our foraged mushroom risotto, every plate is an adventure.

KENYAN CUISINE

Main Dishes (Stews & Grilled)

Nyama Choma:

The national dish of Kenya. Juicy, grilled goat meat seasoned with salt and spices. Often served with a side of kachumbari and ugali.

Kenyan Stew (Mchuzi):

A hearty and flavorful stew, usually made with beef, chicken, or goat, and cooked in a rich tomato-based sauce with potatoes, carrots, and other vegetables.

Rice & Accompaniments

Pilau:

Fragrant rice dish cooked with aromatic spices like cardamom, cloves, cumin, and cinnamon.7 It's often served with a meat stew or kachumbari.

Wali wa Nazi:

White rice cooked with grated coconut milk, giving it a rich and fragrant taste. Popular in the coastal regions.

Chapati:

A soft, layered, unleavened flatbread.9 It's a popular accompaniment to stews and can also be enjoyed on its own.

Mukimo

A staple from the Mount Kenya region, made from mashed potatoes and greens, often mixed with maize and/or beans.
